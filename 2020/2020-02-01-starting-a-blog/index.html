2020 February 1st

The start of it all

I spend a lot of time 'playing' with tech and try to learn as much a possible. The idea of the blog is to help me remember usual tech bits that I have learnt. After a few busy days in work its very difficult to recall the fun stuff I was looking at. I also hope that anyone reading this will also find it useful.

so that led me to looking at blog sites

Wanting to learn while maintaining a blog site was important. I also wanted to focus on the same dev stack that I had been using in work; so a blog site using npm, node, react was something I was looking for. I came across gatsbyjs and decided to play.

There were many tutorials for creating a blogging site, but it was always based on a couple of commands from someone else's theme without really understand how I could evolve it myself. Until I came across Scott Spence post on freecodecamp. It started from nothing that with lots of explanation. Thanks Scott.

I might change to a ready made template, but I feel that spending time really understanding whats running a blog site would be beneficial.
